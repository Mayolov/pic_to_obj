Photo → 3D Mesh

Upload a photo or use your camera — the app estimates depth and builds a downloadable 3D mesh (OBJ & GLB) ready for Blender or OpenGL.

⚠ Limitation: This uses AI depth estimation, not full photogrammetry. The result is a depth-displaced plane mesh — single-sided, best for objects photographed head-on with good lighting and a plain background.
